# Laboratory tests

## Why labs are used

Laboratory tests help the doctor to:

- Evaluate health
- Identify risks
- Monitor organs
- Guide treatment
- Monitor hormones
- Create individualized plans
- Prepare PCT (when relevant)

## Critical rule

**Laboratory tests DO NOT eliminate all risks.**

Every organism reacts differently.
Never guarantee safety based on labs alone.
Never say “if labs are normal, everything is 100% safe.”

## How to explain to patients

- Labs give important information for safer, more individualized decisions.
- They reduce uncertainty; they do not remove all risk.
- The doctor interprets results and builds the plan after review (often at the second consultation).
